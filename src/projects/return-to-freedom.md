Website redesign for Return to Freedom, a nonprofit organization dedicated to protecting America's wild horses and burros through sanctuary care, scientific conservation, policy advocacy, and public education.

## Overview

Return to Freedom maintains sanctuary facilities in California where displaced wild horses and burros receive lifelong care. Their work emphasizes humane fertility control, sustainable land management, and protection of rare historic mustang bloodlines as alternatives to government roundups.

## Project Status

This project is currently underway. The staging deployment shows the work in progress.

## Technical Details

- Built with Next.js and TypeScript
- Modern redesign of their existing website
- Focus on storytelling and donation conversion
